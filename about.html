About me

Hello! My name is Paulina and I am a matcha enthusiast. I began my matcha journey about eight years ago, but the beginning was not easy. I felt overwhelmed and confused by the amount of information available out there, and it has taken me years to master the art of making matcha at home. This year I decided to make it my mission to simplify the process of making matcha for the ladies who want to master the craft and make it a part of their daily ritual, but do not know where to begin. Happy matcha-making!
